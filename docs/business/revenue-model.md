# Revenue Projection Model (Year 1-3)

## 📌 Executive Summary

EduPlay Pro projects to reach **$100M ARR** by the end of Year 3, driven by a land-and-expand strategy targeting districts.

| Metric | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Schools** | 100 | 500 | 2,000 |
| **ARR** | ₹50L ($60k) | ₹2.5Cr ($300k) | ₹10Cr ($1.2M) |
| **Growth** | - | 500% | 400% |

> *Note: Projections above are conservative estimates based on ₹4,999/mo ARPU.*

## 🏗️ Assumptions

1.  **Pricing**:
    *   Starter: ₹0/mo (Freemium Entry)
    *   School: ₹4,999/mo
    *   District: ₹19,999/mo
2.  **Conversion**:
    *   Trial to Paid: 30%
    *   Freemium to Paid: 5%
3.  **Retention**:
    *   Monthly Churn: < 5%
    *   Net Revenue Retention (NRR): 120% (via upsells)

## 📈 Growth Scenarios

### Conservative
*   Organic growth only.
*   10 new schools/month.
*   **Year 3 ARR: ₹4Cr**

### Realistic (Target)
*   Direct sales team (2 reps).
*   Partnership with 3 major districts.
*   **Year 3 ARR: ₹10Cr**

### Aggressive
*   VC Funding ($2M Seed).
*   National marketing campaign.
*   **Year 3 ARR: ₹25Cr+**

## 💰 Unit Economics

*   **LTV (Lifetime Value)**: ₹4,999 × 24 months = **₹1,20,000**
*   **CAC (Acquisition Cost)**: ₹15,000 (Sales commission + Ads)
*   **LTV:CAC Ratio**: **8:1** (Healthy SaaS metric is 3:1)
