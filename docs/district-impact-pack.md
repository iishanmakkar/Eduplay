EduPlay is an Adaptive Cognitive Mastery Engine designed for scale. By leveraging Bayseian Knowledge Tracing (BKT) and psychometric modeling (IRT), EduPlay transforms unstructured practice into precision learning paths, allowing districts to demonstrably close skill gaps and recover lost instructional time.
