# EduPlay Control Group Experiment Evaluation Framework

**Purpose:**  
To establish a statistically rigorous Proof of Impact by comparing traditional instructional methods with the EduPlay Adaptive Cognitive Mastery Engine.

## Experiment Configuration

- **Target Audience:** [Grade Level] [Subject] Classes
- **Duration:** 8 Weeks (1 Marking Term)
- **Sample Size Setup:** 
  - **Group A (Control):** 50% of classes (Using standard LMS/Worksheets)
  - **Group B (Treatment):** 50% of classes (Using EduPlay Adaptive for 45 mins/week)
- **Curriculum Alignment:** Both groups cover the exact same standard outcomes over the 8 weeks.

## Independent & Dependent Variables

### Independent
- **Intervention:** Exposure to EduPlay Adaptive AI (BKT/IRT models) vs Traditional non-adaptive practice.

### Dependent (Measured Outcomes)
1. **Outcome Delta (Summative):** Pre-test (Week 1) vs Post-test (Week 8) raw score gains.
2. **Time-to-Mastery:** Active learning minutes required to achieve 85%+ proficiency across standard clusters.
3. **Engagement Persistence:** Completion rates of assigned formative homework/drills.
4. **Skill Decay Resilience:** Assessment of 30-day retention on early topics evaluated in Week 8.

## Execution Protocol

### Week 1: Establish Baseline
- All students execute a rigorous, standardized pre-test without adaptive assistance.
- Calibrate IRT item difficulties using combined class data.

### Weeks 2-7: Divergent Intervention
- **Group A:** Receives standard paper or linear digital homework.
- **Group B:** Receives EduPlay assignments; AI auto-adjusts difficulty, interleaving weaker concepts for retrieval practice.

### Week 8: Blind Assessment
- All students execute the post-test under identical conditions.
- Psychometricians blind to group assignment run the Impact Measurement Engine script across the final datasets.
