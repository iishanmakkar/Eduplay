# EduPlay 90-Day Pilot — Complete Execution Plan
**Classification: Founder-Internal**
Last Updated: 2026-02-21

---

## The Single Objective

> Convert EduPlay's 96/100 technical readiness into:
> - 10 paying schools
> - 3,000+ active students
> - 3 months of mastery data
> - 1 publishable impact report
> - Clear NRR expansion evidence for Series A

---

## Pilot Parameters

| Parameter | Target |
|---|---|
| Schools | 10 schools (8 STARTER tier, 2 SCHOOL tier) |
| Students | 1,000–3,000 |
| Teachers | 100–200 |
| Region | India only (1 state: Maharashtra or Karnataka) |
| Duration | 90 days (free). Day 91 = renewal decision |
| Pilot price | ₹0 for 90 days |
| Post-pilot target | ₹60,000–₹1,80,000/year per school |
| Primary metric | Mastery delta: avg P(L) growth per student |

---

## School Selection Criteria

**Target profile**: CBSE/ICSE schools, Grades 4–8, 150–600 students.

| Must-Have | Nice-to-Have |
|---|---|
| Active WhatsApp parent group | Principal who shares results publicly |
| At least 1 enthusiastic math teacher | School newsletter / social media presence |
| Computer lab or 1:1 device access | District/trust with multiple branches |
| Willingness to share anonymized data | Competition history (Math Olympiad etc.) |

**Acquisition channel priority**:
1. **Warm intros** (founder → principal network) — fastest activation
2. **Teacher community groups** (WhatsApp, Telegram math teacher groups) — highest volume
3. **District education office** (1 connection → 10 schools) — highest leverage

---

## 90-Day Phased Structure

| Phase | Days | Focus | Key Deliverable |
|---|---|---|---|
| Setup | 0–7 | Onboard 10 schools, activate teachers | 100 teachers active |
| Activation | 8–30 | Get 80% of students to play 3+ sessions | Week 4 WAU > 500 |
| Data collection | 30–60 | Mastery data accumulates; weekly reports | Mastery Growth Reports |
| AI calibration | 60–75 | Use real data to sharpen IRT model | Hardest-skill heatmap |
| Conversion | 75–90 | Present ROI, offer paid tier | 10 signed contracts |

---

## Critical Success Metrics

| Metric | Target by Day 90 |
|---|---|
| WAU (Weekly Active Users) | ≥ 2,400 (80% of 3,000 students) |
| Avg session streak (students) | ≥ 5 days |
| Teacher WAU | ≥ 80 (80% of 100 teachers) |
| Avg mastery P(L) growth | ≥ +0.25 per skill practiced |
| Skill gap closure rate | ≥ 60% of identified weak skills improving |
| School admin open rate (weekly email) | ≥ 40% |
| Day-90 paid conversion rate | ≥ 80% (8/10 schools renew) |
