# Series A Tech Defense Script

**Purpose**: Bulletproof answers for top-tier VCs digging into EduPlay's proprietary tech depth during technical due-diligence.

---

### Q: Why not Duolingo?
**A:** "Duolingo is a B2C gamified sequencer optimized for daily habit streaks, not deep mastery. They map rote memorization. EduPlay maps K-12 cognitive gaps using a proprietary BKT+IRT hybrid system, built directly for B2B district procurement compliance. We aren't competing for user screen-time; we are replacing district benchmark assessments."

### Q: Why not Khan Academy?
**A:** "Khan Academy is a massive, static content tree. Their AI 'Khanmigo' is a conversational tutor taped onto their legacy videos. EduPlay generates its *own* content procedurally in real-time. Our system scales infinitely without needing content creators. An EduPlay student never sees the same problem twice, and the difficulty constantly shifts to match their precise logit ability."

### Q: Why not just GPT wrappers?
**A:** "A basic GPT wrapper hallucinates. If you ask a GPT wrapper to generate a 4th-grade math problem, it will occasionally invent impossible numbers or output a 7th-grade concept. EduPlay passes all AI outputs through a deterministic Abstract Syntax Tree (AST) evaluator and physics simulator to prove correctness *mathematically* and *pedagogically* before rendering it. This makes our platform hallucination-proof, solving the single biggest problem in EdTech AI adoption."

### Q: Why not LMS plugins? (Canvas/Schoology)
**A:** "LMS platforms are filing cabinets. They hold PDFs and gradebooks. EduPlay is a real-time cognitive engine. We integrate *with* LMS instances via LTI 1.3 to push grades backward, but the actual real-time telemetry (the 50ms latency multiplayer scaling, the Redis leaderboards, the BKT matrices) is computationally impossible to run inside an LMS."

### Q: What is proprietary about your tech stack?
**A:** "Our knowledge graph. We map 500+ micro-skills across Bloom's Taxonomy and feed a continuous stream of failure data back into our IRT model. Every time a student misses a step, our model gets smarter about *why* they missed it. We have tenant-isolated psychometric datasets secured over Neon postgres and edge-routed locally via Vercel. Our architecture hits 99.9% uptime SLAs effortlessly."

### Q: Why will districts actually switch and buy this?
**A:** "Return on human capital. Traditional grading and linear differentiation consumes 15-20 hours of a teacher's week. EduPlay automates tier-1 differentiation entirely. Our Dashboard shows precise weakness clusters instantly. At $20/student, we recover $750/student in diverted teacher intervention hours."
